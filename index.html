Legal Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Customer (Buyer) Agreement

By placing an order on Bold.ng, you agree to provide accurate delivery and contact information. All payments made online via credit card or bank transfer are processed securely using PCI-DSS certified banking infrastructure.

Dispute Resolution: Upon delivery, buyers have a specified window to report discrepancies. Bold.ng acts as an impartial mediator to ensure fairness. Money is not fully cleared to unverified merchants until successful delivery is reasonably confirmed.

2. Vendor (Seller) Agreement & Paystack Subaccounts

To ensure absolute financial transparency, Bold.ng utilizes Paystack Subaccounts for our verified merchants. This acts as an automated, secure routing system.

  • Trust & Payouts: Vendors are assured that customer funds are securely captured and will be remitted to their linked bank accounts seamlessly after successful order fulfillment minus the agreed platform commission.
  • Authenticity Mandate: Vendors are legally bound to deliver products exactly as photographed and described. Selling counterfeit, illegal, or misrepresented goods will result in immediate suspension, forfeiture of pending payouts, and potential legal action.

3. Affiliates & Promoters

Affiliates earn commissions strictly on completed, delivered, and non-refunded sales generated through their unique tracking parameters. Bold.ng reserves the right to withhold affiliate payouts if fraudulent traffic or deceptive advertising practices are detected.

4. Platform Liability & Security

Bold.ng provides the technological infrastructure to connect premium buyers with elite sellers. While we enforce strict vetting and buyer protection protocols, our liability is strictly limited to the transaction amount of the specific order in dispute. We employ bank-grade encryption to ensure your personal and payment data is never compromised.
